Design a professional, modern website for a national nonprofit advocacy organization called the "Military Enlisted Association of America (MEAA)".

The mission of MEAA is to advocate for the interests of enlisted military service members and their families at the national policy level in Washington DC. The organization focuses on protecting enlisted pay, benefits, healthcare, housing, transition support, and quality of life.

This organization serves enlisted members from all branches of the United States military: Army, Marine Corps, Navy, Air Force, Space Force, and Coast Guard.

The site should feel authoritative and credible, similar to established national advocacy organizations like MOAA (Military Officers Association of America), but with a modern design and strong patriotic tone.

Design the site with a clean government-adjacent aesthetic: strong typography, clear messaging, bold hero sections, and minimal clutter.

Primary branding themes:
- Professional
- Patriotic but not overly political
- Trustworthy
- National advocacy organization
- Representing enlisted voices

Color palette inspiration:
- Deep Navy (#0B1F3A)
- Military Gold (#C9A227)
- White
- Light Gray backgrounds

Typography style:
- Strong headline serif or modern military-inspired type
- Clean sans-serif body text

Use high-quality imagery of enlisted service members from multiple branches (diverse ranks, backgrounds, and roles). Imagery should show real military life, families, and service.
